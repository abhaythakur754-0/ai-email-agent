## General Market Research

### Customer Support Statistics (for personalization)
- Average support agent salary in India: ₹25,000-40,000/month
- Companies with 500+ tickets/day typically have 20-50 support agents
- Average response time expectation: < 2 hours
- Cost per ticket: ₹150-300 (human agent)
- AI can reduce cost to ₹15-30 per ticket (90% savings)

### Common Pain Points Across All Targets
1. High agent turnover (30-50% annually)
2. Training new agents takes 4-6 weeks
3. Peak hours overflow (lunch times, weekends)
4. Multi-language support needed (Hindi + English)
5. Chat vs Email vs Phone channels
6. Refund/complaint handling speed
7. Integration with existing tools (Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom)

### Target Personas to Contact
- VP of Customer Experience
- Head of Customer Support
- Customer Success Manager
- COO (smaller companies)
- Founder/CEO (early-stage startups)

### Best Approach Strategy
1. Start with D2C e-commerce (higher urgency)
2. Target companies that recently raised funding
3. Focus on companies actively hiring support roles
4. Lead with ROI calculator showing 60-70% cost savings
